# Brand: Acme Hot Sauce Co.

## Identity
- **Name:** Acme Hot Sauce Co.
- **Type:** ecom
- **Market:** DE
- **URL:** https://acmehotsauce.example.com

## Voice & Tone
- **Voice:** bold, craft-forward, slightly irreverent
- **Tone keywords:** confident, sensory, curated, surprising, real
- **Avoid:** discount language, superlatives, "world-class", "revolutionary", exclamation marks

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- **Who:** Urban food enthusiasts aged 28-45 who cook at home and care about provenance
- **Pain:** Supermarket hot sauce is boring, same brands everywhere
- **Trigger:** Discovering a new producer or flavour they haven't tried

## Design Tokens
- **Colors:** primary #a41c48 (action red), secondary #111111 (charred black), accent #e3d4b5 (smoky beige)
- **Fonts:** League Spartan (headlines), Inter (body)
- **Aesthetic:** editorial brutalism — bold type, borders not shadows, generous whitespace
- **Component patterns:** border-based cards, oversized typography, stamp-style badges
